Learning Plan

Bootcamp Learning Plan

I see the bootcamp as an opportunity to learn what is necessary to start a new career, as I want to become a senior software developer, be an expert in some software dev skill.

I want to have the opportunity to use the knowledge gained in the bootcamp to get a job as a junior software developer and be able to deliver more than just junior software skills, but also contribute my personal values to the company in which I am offered the opportunity.

I believe that perseverance will be a strong point in my favor. I am aware that I am giving up a lot to do the bootcamp and that I have to use this determination to achieve my goals. On the other hand, the fact that I am not a native English speaker can influence my learning, because the content we are learning is already difficult itself, in a fast and dynamic environment this can make it a little more challenging.

During foundations, I believe that I have gained the ability to accomplish what I have to learn at each moment instead of wanting to learn everything at once.

Something that I hope to evolve during the bootcamp is my self-confidence to learn and also the ability to work under pressure and deal with it. When I leave something behind, I have difficulty stopping thinking about it and it doesn't add anything to the situation. I also want to learn how to have the ability to continue studying after the bootcamp, mainly due to the fact that in the Tech industry, we must always be a student and updating ourselves. I hope to adopt this habit.

In terms of support, I believe that it should be provided at any time when identified that it's important. Sometimes people don't even notice that they need support, because they are too focused on their routine. I hope that if it is identified by the bootcamp that some type of support is needed, it will be said.

I hope to give 100% of myself during this period to do the most of it, I commit to being patient and having a Team Work attitude towards my colleagues.
